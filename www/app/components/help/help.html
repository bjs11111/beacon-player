The Beacon Experimenting Tool

BeaconPlayer is a free Open Source Platform and App for experimenting with Beacons.
Its functionality is simple and easy: Whenever you approach a registered beacon with your smartphone, the app loads the related media content from the Content Management Platform. Everything is fully configurable in a few steps.

QR Code Reader

BeaconPlayer includes a QR Code Reader. Manage your QR Codes the same way as you manage your Beacons.
