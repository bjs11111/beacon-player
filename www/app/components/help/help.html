BeaconPlayer

The Beacon Experimenting Tool

BeaconPlayer is a free and Open Source Platform and App for experimenting with beacons.

It's functionality is simple: whenever you approach a beacon with your phone, the app loads the appropriate media content for it. Everythings fully configurable in few quick steps.
